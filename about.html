Our Mission

At Patricia Real Estate, our mission is simple: to make property ownership accessible, transparent and rewarding — whether you're searching for a family home in the UK, an apartment in Spain, or investing in land in Ecuador.

We believe that finding or selling a property should be a positive, empowering experience. That means honest advice, clear communication, and a team that genuinely cares about your outcome — not just the transaction.

With operations across three countries and a multilingual team, we bring local expertise and global perspective to every client relationship.

Our Story

Patricia Real Estate was founded on a simple belief: that property — wherever in the world — should be accessible to everyone, handled with care and backed by expertise. What started as a local estate agency in London has grown into an international property group with deep roots in three countries.

2016

Founded in London, UK

Patricia AM opens the first office, focusing on residential lettings across London.

2019

Expansion to Spain

Growing demand from international buyers leads to a dedicated Spain portfolio, with listings across Spain.

2021

Ecuador Land & Investment Division

Partnering with local developers, Patricia Real Estate launches its Ecuador division — helping overseas Ecuadorians and international investors access high-growth land projects on the coast.

2026

A Global Property Group

Today, Patricia Real Estate serves clients across three continents, with a multilingual team and a track record of hundreds of successful transactions.
